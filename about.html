About us

Refugee Stories helps visitors gain a better understanding of the refugee crisis and what it means to be a refugee. There are more people displaced in the world today than at any time since the end of World War II and it is more important than ever to help people develop empathy for each other. We aim to make people’s stories heard, while providing them with a sense of community and belonging. By connecting volunteers with people in need, we can change the world, one story at a time.
